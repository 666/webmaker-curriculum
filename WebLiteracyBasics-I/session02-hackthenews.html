2. Writing the Web


Activity A: Hack the News

Made by MOUSE and remixed for Clubs by Mozilla.

Learners will use X-Ray Goggles to remix a news website, learning about openly-licensed resources, different forms of media, and how to create something new on the Web.

  • Preparation

    1. Complete the pre-activity questionnaire.
    2. Do the activity on your own to become familiar with it.

  • 10
    min

    Introduction

    Welcome your students and explain that today we're going to explore "remix." Ask a few students to say what they think of when they hear "remix." Share some popular examples from your local context.

    Now invite the students to pair up. Give each student a piece of paper and a pencil.

    Ask them to draw their favorite fictional character. Don't worry about accuracy or too much detail -- just sketch it quickly.

    After a few minutes, have the pairs swap their drawings. Now prompt the pairs to add something to their partner's drawing. This is remix in action!

  • 25
    min

    Hack the News using X-Ray Goggles

    In the same pairs, if enough equipment is available, have the students open a modern web browser.
