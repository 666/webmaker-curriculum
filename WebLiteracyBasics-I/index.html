Welcome to Club Testing!

Thanks for joining in the experiment. Here are the activities we're testing for local clubs and info on the process.

  • The Testing Process

    We recommend 45min. to teach each activity with prep time to try it yourself first.

    There are also brief pre & post questionnaires for you and your learners to complete for each activity. Once you've tested both activities, we'll wrap up with a written reflection.

    On [date] we'll have a call to check in with everyone. Email mozteach@mozillafoundation.org at any time. As always, we welcome your feedback to make this process better and work for you!
